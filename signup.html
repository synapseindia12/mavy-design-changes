    What's MAVY?

    Short for Maverick - an independent thinker - MAVY is a platform for Millenials to have their voices heard, opinions understood, and desires met.

    Among other things, MAVY is a virtual community created and managed by millennials that can tell you exactly what they're thinking and a research tool available to marketers who have something to say.
